The 4-Step Intelligent Shield

PureStream sits between the raw social firehose and the client dashboard, acting as a smart filter that uses Natural Language Processing to detect contextual anomalies.

1

High-Volume Ingest

PureStream continuously monitors the live social firehose. It establishes a moving baseline of normal mention volume for every single client query.

Baseline for "Oasis": 200 mentions/day
2

Automated Spike Detection

When a keyword goes viral for the wrong reasons, the system instantly flags the exponential anomaly using dynamic statistical thresholds.

Alert: Volume spiked to 500,000/hr
3

Contextual NLP Scoring

Our NLP engine reads the surrounding text of the spike. It compares the new data against the client's historical brand context.

One-Click Resolution

Low-confidence data is quarantined. Analysts get a clear alert to "Approve" or "Block". PureStream auto-generates the boolean exclusion rules.
